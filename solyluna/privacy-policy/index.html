Solyluna Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Solyluna app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Luis Fariña Lázaro (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is provided "AS IS".


What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application acquires the information you supply when you download the Application. When you contact the Service Provider through the Application for support, feedback, or any other purpose, you provide information that offers helpful context for your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

The Service Provider does not collect or store any information.

The Application displays advertisements provided by Unity. All advertisements are non-targeted in order to conform to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For more information, please refer to Unity's Privacy Policy. No personal information is stored. This applies to all users worldwide.


Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.


Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

The Application does not transmit any personal information. The Service Provider will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

Please note that the Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policy about handling data. Below are the links to the Privacy Policy of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

The Service Provider initializes the Unity Ads SDK with the parameter “gdpr.consent” set to "false", so that advertisements are non-targeted and no personal information is transmitted.


Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

If you would like the Service Provider to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Application, please contact them at contact@luisfl.me and they will respond in a reasonable time.


Children

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided them with information, please contact the Service Provider (contact@luisfl.me) so that they will be able to take the necessary actions.


Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information they process and maintain. For example, they do not collect personal information from users, and no personal information is disclosed to any third party. Please be aware that, although they endeavor to provide reasonable security for information they process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.


Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
