Wordots Privacy Policy

We don't collect or store any of your data. Your Dots and stats are stored locally. We can't access this data.

If you sign up for Wordots Pro, all billing, payment processing, and subscription management are handled by the App Store. The only data available to us is related to the subscription status and is not shared with anyone.

When you use the "Add Dot with AI" feature, the app sends the data provided in the form to our server. Once the request is fulfilled, the data is discarded.

If you've got any questions or comments, feel free to reach out to us: contact@luisfl.me
